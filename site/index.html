    Using Advanced Technology for Our Eye Correction Surgery

    At NewVision Clinics, we use only the latest and most advanced laser eye surgery technology available. As the industry evolves, so does the technology our laser eye surgery specialists use. We are constantly refining our laser treatments to give you the best outcomes imaginable.

    Read more
    • VisX STAR S4 (Cheltenham)
    • Schwind Amaris 1050RS (East Melbourne)
    • Elita Femtosecond Laser (Cheltenham)
    • What is WaveScan Technology?
    • Advanced VSS Vector Planning (For the Optimal Treatment of Astigmatism)

    SILK uses the ELITA laser to gently create a small, disc-shaped piece of tissue (lenticule) in the cornea. The lenticule is removed through a microscopic (keyhole) incision, reshaping the cornea and correcting the patients vision, all within minutes.

    The ELITA laser results in an exceptionally fast lenticular procedure (~16 seconds) with next-day results and recovery with smooth corneal surface and clean flap edges, resulting in better healing and exceptional outcomes.

    The ELITA system is the latest precision laser technology available.

    SILK is the newest lenticular procedure and is now available at NewVision Clinics, leading the way in refractive laser surgery.

    Founder & Medical Director

    A global authority in laser eye and cataract surgery, Professor Noel Alpins AM has performed over 40,000 procedures and is internationally recognised for his pioneering work in astigmatism treatment.     Dr Lana Del Porto
    Cataract, Refractive & Strabismus Surgeon

    Dr Lana Del Porto is a fellowship-trained ophthalmic surgeon with expertise in cataract, laser vision correction, and squint surgery. She trained at Moorfields Eye Hospital in London and now teaches cataract surgery at the Royal Victorian Eye and Ear Hospital. She is known for her precise technique, academic contributions, and patient-centred care.
